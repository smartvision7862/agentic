Connected accounts

Connect via Zernio. Only Instagram, LinkedIn, and X are enabled.

Setup notes
  • Instagram: requires a Business or Creator account. Zernio runs the Meta OAuth and handles container creation and publishing.
  • LinkedIn: personal or company page via Zernio OAuth.
  • X / Twitter: OAuth via Zernio (platform value twitter).
  • After connecting, click Sync accounts to pull them in.

Gmail (mail & expenses)

Connect Gmail (read-only) to fetch recent mail and auto-extract income & expenses on the dashboard.

Background poll to refresh mail & expenses. Minimum 1 hour; set 0 to turn off.

Setup notes
  • Add GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID and GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET to .env (see .env.example).
  • In Google Cloud, enable the Gmail API and add redirect URI http://localhost:3950/api/gmail/callback.
  • Scope used is gmail.readonly only.
